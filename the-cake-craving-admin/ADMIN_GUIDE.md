## ğŸ° Adding & Editing Products

### To Add a New Product:

1. **Click "Products"** in the left sidebar
2. **Click the green "+ Create" button** (top right)
3. **Fill in the details:**

   - **Product Name**: e.g., "Chocolate Truffle Cake"
   - **Description**: Brief description of the cake
   - **Product Image**: Click "Upload" and select a photo from your computer
   - **Cake Type**: Choose one:
     - Chocolate (for chocolate-based cakes)
     - Classic (for vanilla, butterscotch, etc.)
     - Theme (for birthday, wedding themes)
     - Fruit (for pineapple, mango, etc.)
   - **Best Seller**: Check this box if it's one of your popular cakes
   - **Display Order**: Use numbers 1, 2, 3... to control the order (lower numbers appear first)

4. **Click "Publish"** (bottom right)

Your new product is now live on the website! ğŸŠ

### To Edit an Existing Product:

1. Click "Products" in the left sidebar
2. Click on the product you want to edit
3. Make your changes
4. Click "Publish"

### To Delete a Product:

1. Click "Products" in the left sidebar
2. Click on the product

## â­ Managing Testimonials

### To Add a Customer Review:

1. **Click "Testimonials"** in the left sidebar
2. **Click "+ Create"**
3. **Fill in:**
   - **Customer Name**: e.g., "Priya Sharma"
   - **Testimonial Text**: What they said about your cake
   - **Rating**: 1-5 stars (usually 5!)
   - **Display Order**: 1, 2, 3... (lower appears first)
4. **Click "Publish"**

### Tips for Good Testimonials:
- Keep them short and sweet (2-3 sentences)
- Include specific details ("chocolate truffle cake")
- Always ask permission before posting customer feedback

## ğŸ¨ Editing Hero Section

The Hero Section is the **big banner** at the top of your homepage.

### To Change It:

1. **Click "Hero Section"** in the left sidebar
2. **Edit:**
   - **Main Title**: The big headline
   - **Subtitle**: The description below the title
   - **Button Text**: Text on the main button (usually "Order Now")
   - **Background Image**: Upload a beautiful cake photo

3. **Click "Publish"**

### Best Practices:
- Use a high-quality, appetizing cake photo
- Keep the title short and catchy
- Make sure the photo is bright and clear

### Common Issues:

**Q: I can't see my changes on the website**
- A: Did you click "Publish"? If yes, wait 1-2 minutes and refresh the page.

**Q: My photo isn't uploading**
- A: Make sure the file is JPG or PNG, and under 10MB in size.

**Q: I accidentally deleted something**
- A: Contact support immediately. We can help restore it.

**Q: Can I preview before publishing?**
- A: Yes! Click "Save Draft" first, then check the preview in Sanity Studio.
